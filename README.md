Introduction

This project is based on digital literacy and focuses on understanding how to use digital tools in a safe and effective way. It includes different tasks related to online platforms, communication, and cyber safety.

---

Task 1 – Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. It highlights important points like safe internet usage, useful digital tools, and responsible online behavior.

---

Task 2 – Portfolio

In this task, I created my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online presence.

---

Task 3 – Platforms

In this task, I explored HackerRank and created a quiz using Google Forms. This helped me understand how online platforms can be used for learning and awareness.

---

Task 4 – Email and Etiquette

In this task, I wrote professional emails, created a list of do’s and don’ts for social media, and learned about proper digital communication.

---

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I learned about phishing and created a case study, safety checklist, and reflection. This helped me understand the importance of online safety.

---

Conclusion

This project helped me improve my understanding of digital tools, online safety, and professional communication. It was a useful learning experience that will help me in the future.
